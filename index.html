my-playlist

A minimal YouTube playlist shuffler.

Copy this URL: https://kiwijuice56.github.io/my-playlist/?id=[PLAYLIST_ID] and replace [PLAYLIST_ID] with the text after list= in your YouTube playlist URL. The resulting URL will download the collection of video ID's to your local storage and load a shuffled sample (of size 128 due to API limitations) every time you refresh the page. You can bookmark the URL for quick access to your playlist. Enable desktop site on firefox mobile to stop audio from pausing when switching tabs. Click here to refresh your playlist after adding/removing songs. Due to a YouTube bug, you may notice the page refresh itself a few times when first loading a playlist.

Zero data is collected from users. This website was made to remove the bloat of the YouTube website for playing music in the background while also fixing the broken shuffle feature.
