my-playlist

a minimal youtube playlist controller. copy this url: https://kiwijuice56.github.io/my-playlist/?id=[PLAYLIST_ID]&shuffle=1 and replace [PLAYLIST_ID] with the text after list= in your youtube playlist url. remove shuffle=1 to disable shuffling. after entering your url, you can play, pause, and skip using keyboard controls or the buttons below the video. you can also bookmark the url to save your settings.

zero data is collected from users. this website was made to remove the bloat of the youtube website and fix bugs with shuffling. the back and next buttons were added for mobile support, as these buttons are missing on firefox android for some reason.
